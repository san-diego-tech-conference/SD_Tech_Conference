SD Undergrad Tech Conference

The San Diego Undergraduate Tech Conference is a student-run event designed to celebrate the creativity and innovation of undergraduate work in computing and data sciences.

A space where students can showcase passion projects, establish a startup, and dive deeper into research.

The conference event gives undergrads experience practicing technical public speaking and networking, while the papers provide publication opportunities.

Our Mission

  • Empowering Undergraduates – Providing a structured and accessible platform for students to develop, present, and discuss their work.
  • Encouraging Collaboration – Fostering a dynamic community of thinkers, builders, and innovators.
  • Ensuring Fair Recognition – Through a blind review process with experienced UCSD tutors lecturers, every submission is evaluated on its merit.
